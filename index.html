Projects

GPA Calculator

This GPA calculator is one of my first projects. It was made specifically for Prosper High School and has a unique feature which allows users to directly paste their transcript to calculate their GPA- opposed to manually inputing all of their grades. It works by spliting the contents of the transcript to seperate class name, weight, and grade. From there, it makes the proper calculations based on the Prosper ISD handbook and outputs a weighted GPA- highlighting possible incorrect inputs in red for the user to manually fix.
