Varsity Football
The Varsity Football program at Prosper High School goes beyond just football; it fosters growth in social, academic, and athletic areas through four core values: Service to others, honor the team, belief in the process, and expecting excellence. Personally, the program has instilled hard work and discipline in me, motivating me to contribute more to my community. I appreciate and partake in many of the extra initiatives like kids training camps, the Flight Crew program, Elementary Car Doors, and various volunteering opportunities that help strengthen our community.
