What makes this platform unique is that the lessons are taught by a fellow student, offering a fresh perspective on problem-solving and learning. By approaching challenges from a student’s point of view, I provide relatable and practical solutions that stick with my audience. Custom Made GPA Calculator
By leveraging AI along with my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I developed a custom GPA calculator specifically designed for my high school. The inspiration came from my own experience of trying to calculate my GPA on other websites, which were time-consuming and required manually entering each class and grade. What sets my calculator apart is its ability to automatically extract data from a student’s transcript. Instead of manually inputting information, users can simply paste their transcript, and the calculator will instantly populate the table and compute their GPA. This streamlined process makes GPA calculation up to five times faster and significantly reduces hassle.
